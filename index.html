Naruto, and Naruto Shippuden is about a boy named Naruto who's parents died when fighting a man named Obito and also the nine tailed beast Kurama. Naruto goes through most of his life being a jinchuriki who people mainly fear because of the beast that they have sealed within them. I really like this show and it is put in my number two spot because of how strong Naruto is. Even though sometimes it may seem that everyone is against him he still keeps fighting to get stronger and achieve his dream to become hokage. All the time he pushes past his limit fighting along his time and getting stronger. This shows people never to give up even if its impossible you should always try to make it possible and when you fail get back up and go beyond PLUS ULTRA.
